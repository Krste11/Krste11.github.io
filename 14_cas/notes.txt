Positioning elements 

normal flow - every element appears on a new line

relative positoning - it can moved top right left and bottom

absolute positoning - in relation to its containing element 

fixed positoning - this is a form of absulute positioning that positions the element 

floating elements - far left or right

position sticky - according to the normal flow of the document

overlaping elements - z-index
